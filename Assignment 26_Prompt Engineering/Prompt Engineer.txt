Assignment (30/03/2026)
Assignment Name: Prompt Engineer
1. Resume Prompt
Weak Prompt:
“Write a resume.”
Strong Prompt:
“Create a professional resume for a Computer Science student with skills in Python, Machine Learning, and Web Development. Include education, projects, technical skills, and internships in a clean format.”
2. Business Idea Prompt
Weak Prompt:
“Give me a business idea.”
Strong Prompt:
“Suggest a low-investment tech-based business idea for college students in India, including target audience, revenue model, and growth potential.”
3. Study Plan Prompt
Weak Prompt:
“Make a study plan.”
Strong Prompt:
“Create a 4-week study plan for learning Python basics, including daily topics, practice tasks, and revision schedule for a beginner.”
Comparison (Weak vs Strong Prompts)
Weak prompts are vague and lack details, leading to generic answers.
Strong prompts are specific, detailed, and goal-oriented, producing more accurate and useful responses.
Strong prompts clearly define context, requirements, and expected output.
Learning Outcomes
Understand the importance of clear and specific prompts.
Learn how to improve output quality using prompt engineering.
Develop skills in writing effective instructions for AI systems.
Recognize the difference between generic and structured queries.
Enhance ability to interact efficiently with AI tools.
