Assignment Name: Semantic Meaning
Word Pairs with Semantic Similarity
Happy – Joyful
Both words express a feeling of happiness and positive emotion.
Big – Large
These words have similar meanings related to size or magnitude.
Fast – Quick
Both describe speed or something happening in a short time.
Smart – Intelligent
These words refer to a person’s ability to think and understand well.
Angry – Furious
Both indicate strong feelings of anger, with "furious" being more intense.
Explanation

Semantic similarity refers to how closely related the meanings of words are. Words with similar meanings can often be used interchangeably depending on context, although slight differences in intensity or usage may exist.

Learning Outcomes
Understand the concept of semantic similarity in language.
Learn how words can have similar meanings but different intensities.
Improve vocabulary and understanding of word relationships.
Gain basic knowledge useful for NLP and language models.
Develop skills in analyzing meaning and context in language.
