Assignment Name: AI Around Me
Google Search – Uses ML to rank search results based on relevance and past user behavior using prediction models.
YouTube Recommendations – ML analyzes watch history and preferences to suggest videos using recommendation algorithms.
Google Maps Navigation – Uses ML to predict traffic and find the fastest routes based on real-time data.
Voice Assistants (Google Assistant/Siri) – Uses speech recognition and NLP models to understand voice commands and respond.
Email Spam Filter – ML classifies emails as spam or important by learning patterns from past emails.
Face Unlock in Smartphones – Uses computer vision and deep learning to recognize facial features.
Netflix Recommendations – ML suggests movies/shows based on viewing history and user similarity.
Social Media Feed (Instagram/Facebook) – ML prioritizes posts based on user interactions and interests.
Online Shopping Suggestions (Amazon/Flipkart) – Uses ML to recommend products based on browsing and purchase history.
Autocorrect & Predictive Text – ML predicts the next word by learning language patterns from large datasets.
